Fractals

This is the website for my thesis project "Fractals and their application in game design".
Scroll down to discover more.

The Paper

The core of the project is the thesis paper. In it different 2-, 3- and n-dimensional fractals are mathematically described and studied. In addition, it analyses different technologies, that are used to render fractals, and precisely documents how the fractals have been programmed. Finally, it discusses different applications of fractals in different areas, with focus on game design.
